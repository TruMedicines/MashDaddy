50 Mixer and Pestle Recipes Mash Daddy

The Mortar and Pestle remains a crucial tool in culinary traditions around the world, and it deserves to be

treated as an essential in every kitchen. Not just because it served our ancestors for 35,000 years, but

because it continues to do what no other item in the kitchen does: smashing fibers and cells apart to

fundamentally transform their texture and release their full aroma and flavor. That's something a knife

blade cannot do.

Guacamole

Makes 4 1/2 cups Servings

5 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro, divided

1/4 cup minced white onion

1 large jalapeño, seeded, minced, divided

Pinch of Kosher salt

6 medium or 4 large avocados halved, pitted

1 medium tomato, cored, chopped

3 Tbsp. fresh lime juice

1 tsp. lime zest

Combine 2 1/2 Tbsp. cilantro, onion, and 1 Tbsp. jalapeño in a Kitchen Aid bowl , season with salt - leave

mixer head unlocked - turn on mixer until a coarse paste forms. Scoop cubes of avacado into bowl with

onion mixture. Mix in remaining cilantro and jalapeño along with the tomato, lime juice, and zest. Season

to taste with salt. Serve with tortilla chips. DO AHEAD Can be made 4 hours ahead. Press plastic wrap on

surface of guacamole and chill.
